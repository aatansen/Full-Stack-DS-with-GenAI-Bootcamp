## 2. Product & Category Analysis

- **Finding**

  - The top-performing products are **Talkmate Max (Black and Blue variants)**, contributing the highest revenue.
  - The **total units sold exceed target units**, indicating **strong demand and sales performance**.
  - Feature phones dominate both **sales volume and revenue**, while smartphones lag behind.
  - Sales are concentrated within the **price range of ৳3,200 to ৳9,200**, showing clear customer preference for **affordable products**.

This suggests that the market is **highly price-sensitive and driven by budget-friendly feature phones**.

- **Recommendation**

  - Increase inventory and promotional efforts for **high-performing products like Talkmate Max**.
  - Expand offerings within the **৳3K–৳9K price range**, as this is the most demanded segment.
  - Introduce **affordable smartphone options** within this range to improve smartphone adoption.
  - Evaluate and optimize **low-performing or high-priced products**.

## 3. Dealer & Regional Insights

- **Finding**

  - **Faruk Traders** is the top-performing dealer, outperforming all others.
  - Other strong dealers include:

    - Hakim Telecom
    - Jahid Mobile Zone
  - The **average units sold per dealer is 53.79**, indicating moderate dealer productivity.
  - Regional distribution shows:

    - **Dhaka has the highest number of shops (7)**
    - **Chittagong and Sylhet have 2 shops each**
    - Other regions have only **1 shop each**

This indicates that sales and dealer presence are **highly concentrated in Dhaka**, with limited expansion in other regions.

- **Recommendation**

  - Strengthen relationships with **top-performing dealers** and replicate their strategies across others.
  - Expand the dealer network in **underrepresented regions** to increase market coverage.
  - Provide **training, incentives, and support** to lower-performing dealers to improve sales.
  - Focus on **regional expansion strategies** beyond Dhaka to drive growth.

## 4. Overall Business Insight

- **Finding**

  - The company is **profitable but not meeting its revenue potential**.
  - Revenue and profit are **highly concentrated in feature phones**, indicating lack of diversification.
  - Smartphone performance is relatively weak despite being a key growth segment.
  - Sales success is driven by **affordability and strong dealer performance in specific regions**.

This shows a business that is **stable but lacks balance and growth diversification**.

- **Recommendation**

  - Diversify product strategy by **investing in affordable smartphones**.
  - Focus on **scaling successful products and pricing strategies**.
  - Expand geographically to **reduce dependency on a single region (Dhaka)**.
  - Align sales and product strategies with **customer affordability trends**.
