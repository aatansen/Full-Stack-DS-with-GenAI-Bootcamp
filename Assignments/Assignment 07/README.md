## 1. Workforce Composition

- **Finding**
  - The company has a total workforce of **409 employees**.
  - The workforce is **highly concentrated in production roles**, with **363 Line Operators** representing the majority of employees.
  - The **Production Department (154 employees)** and **Quality Department (126 employees)** together account for most of the workforce.
  - Employee category distribution shows:
    - **Direct workers: 92.42%**
    - **Indirect workers: 5.87%**
    - **Management: 1.71%**

This indicates that the company operates with a **heavily production-oriented workforce and very lean management structure**.

- **Recommendation**
  - Ensure **adequate supervisory and managerial capacity** to manage the large operational workforce effectively.
  - Consider **strengthening middle-management roles** such as supervisors or team leaders to maintain productivity and quality control.
